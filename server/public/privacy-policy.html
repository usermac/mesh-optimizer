1. Introduction

Welcome to MeshOpt ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our 3D mesh optimization service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy applies to all users of our service at webdeliveryengine.com.

Key Principle: We collect only what's necessary to provide our service. Your 3D files belong to you, and we never use them for any purpose other than processing your request.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected when you purchase credits. Used solely for transaction tracking and providing access to your transaction history.

2.2 Uploaded Content

  • 3D Model Files: OBJ, GLB, GLTF, FBX, and related texture/material files (MTL, JPG, PNG, etc.) that you upload for processing.
  • Processing Parameters: Settings you choose (optimization mode, reduction ratio, output format).

2.3 Transaction Data

  • Credit Purchases: Date, amount, and number of credits purchased.
  • Credit Usage: Date, file processed, and credits spent per transaction.

2.4 Technical Information

  • Session Data: Temporary session tokens for authentication and state management during your browser session.
  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and request timestamps (retained for security and debugging purposes only).

2.5 What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Payment card numbers or financial information
  • ❌ Personal names or physical addresses
  • ❌ Phone numbers
  • ❌ Tracking cookies or behavioral analytics
  • ❌ Social media information
  • ❌ Device identifiers beyond standard web logs

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Process your 3D mesh optimization requests
  • Generate output files in your requested formats
  • Manage your credit balance and transactions
  • Provide access to your transaction history

3.2 Communication

  • Send transaction confirmations (only when credits are purchased or used)
  • Respond to support inquiries if you contact us

3.3 Technical Operations

  • Maintain and improve service performance
  • Debug technical issues
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure security of our systems

3.4 What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ Sell or rent your information to third parties
  • ❌ Use your 3D models for training AI or machine learning
  • ❌ Share your files with other users or companies
  • ❌ Send marketing or promotional emails
  • ❌ Track your behavior across other websites

4. 3D File Handling & Security

⏱️ Automatic Deletion: All uploaded 3D files are automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour after processing completes.

4.1 Processing Workflow

  1. Upload: Your file is uploaded via encrypted SSL connection
  2. Temporary Storage: File is stored in isolated processing queue
  3. Processing: Optimization is performed on our servers
  4. Download: Processed file is made available to you for download
  5. Deletion: Original and processed files are automatically deleted within 1 hour

4.2 File Ownership & Rights

  • You Own Your Files: We claim no ownership, rights, or licenses to any 3D models you upload.
  • No Usage Rights: We do not use, view, analyze, or process your files for any purpose other than providing the optimization service you requested.
  • Confidentiality: Your files are treated as confidential and proprietary to you.

4.3 Encryption

  • In Transit: All file uploads and downloads use SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • At Rest: Files stored temporarily during processing are secured on protected servers

5. Data Retention

5.1 Retention Periods

  • 3D Files: Maximum 1 hour after processing
  • Transaction History: Retained indefinitely for your records and accounting purposes
  • Email Addresses: Retained as long as you have an active account with credits or transaction history
  • Server Logs: Typically 30-90 days for security and debugging

5.2 Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please contact us at the address provided in Section 11. We will delete your email address and transaction history within 30 days, subject to any legal retention requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Payment Processing (Stripe)

We use Stripe to process credit card payments. Stripe handles all payment information, and we never see or store your credit card details.

  • What Stripe Receives: Your email, payment card information, and transaction amount
  • What We Receive: Confirmation that payment succeeded and your email address
  • Stripe's Privacy Policy: stripe.com/privacy

6.2 Hosting & Infrastructure

Our service is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. These providers have access to server data but are bound by confidentiality agreements and do not use your data for any other purpose.

6.3 No Analytics or Tracking

We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking or analytics services.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold (email address and transaction history)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Request export of your transaction history in CSV format (available in the app)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 11.

8. Security Measures

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted
  • Secure Database: Email addresses and transaction records stored in protected database with access controls
  • File Isolation: Uploaded files are processed in isolated environments
  • Automatic Deletion: Files are automatically purged after processing
  • No Permanent File Storage: We don't maintain archives or backups of your uploaded files
  • API Key Authentication: Secure API keys for account access

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

9. Cookies & Tracking

9.1 Session Management Only

We use minimal session storage in your browser to manage authentication state and ensure files are processed one-by-one. This is essential for the application to function.

9.2 No Tracking Cookies

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking
  • Third-party cookies of any kind

9.3 Browser Storage

We use browser localStorage/sessionStorage to temporarily store:

  • Your API key (for authentication)
  • Application state (to prevent multiple simultaneous uploads)

This data never leaves your browser and is cleared when you log out or close your browser session.
