1. Introduction

Welcome to MeshOpt ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your 3D assets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and API services.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Key Promise: We never sell your data or your 3D models. Your files are yours.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected during payment/checkout for billing purposes.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers.

2.2 Usage Data

  • API Usage: We log API requests, timestamps, and file sizes to calculate credit usage and prevent abuse.
  • IP Addresses: Logged for security and rate-limiting purposes.

2.3 User Content (3D Files)

  • Uploads: We temporarily process the 3D files (OBJ, FBX, GLB, etc.) and textures you upload.
  • Derivatives: We generate optimized versions (GLB, USDZ) of your files.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • To provide the 3D optimization service
  • To manage your credit balance
  • To authenticate your API requests

3.2 Communications

  • To send transaction receipts
  • To notify you of critical service updates
  • To respond to your support requests

4. 3D File Handling & Security

Your Intellectual Property: You retain full ownership of all files you upload and the optimized files we generate. We claim no ownership rights.

4.1 Processing

  1. Upload: Files are transferred via secure SSL/TLS encryption.
  2. Processing: Files are processed on isolated workers.
  3. Storage: Input and output files are stored temporarily in a private S3 bucket.

4.2 Access Control

  • No Public Access: Your files are not accessible to the public.
  • Private Links: Download links are signed and expire automatically.

5. Data Retention

5.1 3D Files

  • Auto-Deletion: All uploaded files and generated results are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours.
  • Immediate Deletion: You cannot manually delete files before the 24-hour window, but rest assured they are purged automatically.

5.2 Account Data

We retain billing emails and transaction history indefinitely for tax and accounting purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Payment Processing

  • Stripe: We use Stripe to process payments.
  • What We Receive: Confirmation that payment succeeded and your email address.
  • Stripe's Privacy Policy: stripe.com/privacy

6.2 Hosting & Infrastructure

Our service is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. These providers have access to server data but are bound by confidentiality agreements and do not use your data for any other purpose.

6.3 No Analytics or Tracking

We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking or analytics services.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold (email address and transaction history)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Request export of your transaction history in CSV format (available in the app)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 11.

8. Security Measures

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted
  • Secure Database: Email addresses and transaction records stored in protected database with access controls
  • File Isolation: Uploaded files are processed in isolated environments
  • Automatic Deletion: Files are automatically purged after processing
  • No Permanent File Storage: We don't maintain archives or backups of your uploaded files
  • API Key Authentication: Secure API keys for account access

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

9. Cookies & Tracking

9.1 Session Management Only

We use minimal session storage in your browser to manage authentication state and ensure files are processed one-by-one. This is essential for the application to function.

9.2 No Tracking Cookies

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking
  • Third-party cookies of any kind

9.3 Browser Storage

We use browser localStorage/sessionStorage to temporarily store:

  • Your API key (for authentication)
  • Application state (to prevent multiple simultaneous uploads)

This data never leaves your browser and is cleared when you log out or close your browser session.
