🔒 Quick summary of what we collect and how we use it

What We Collect

  • Email Address: Only to track your credit purchases and transaction history
  • Uploaded 3D Files: Temporarily stored for processing (max 1 hour)
  • Transaction Records: Credits purchased and used (for your history page)

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ No payment card information (handled by Stripe)
  • ❌ No tracking cookies or analytics
  • ❌ No personal names or addresses
  • ❌ No rights to your 3D models

🛡️ Your Files Are Safe: All uploaded 3D models are automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing. We don't use, share, or keep your files for any other purpose. They're yours from start to finish.

Security

  • SSL Encryption: All data transfers are encrypted in transit
  • Secure Storage: Email and transaction data stored in protected database
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data stays with us (except Stripe for payments)
