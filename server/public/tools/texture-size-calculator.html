Understanding Texture Sizes

  • 256px Tiny objects, distant props, icons. ~256KB uncompressed per texture.
  • 512px Small props, mobile games, thumbnails. ~1MB uncompressed per texture.
  • 1024px Standard for most web 3D. Good balance of quality and performance. ~4MB uncompressed.
  • 2048px Hero products, close-up views, desktop-only experiences. ~16MB uncompressed.
  • 4096px Rarely needed for web. Film/archviz only. ~64MB uncompressed - will crash mobile devices.
